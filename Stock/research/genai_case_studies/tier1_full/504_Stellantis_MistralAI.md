## Detailed Narrative

### Strategic Context
Stellantis elevated its partnership with Mistral AI from pilots to company-wide deployment in October 2025, embedding generative AI across vehicle engineering, in-car experiences, and manufacturing. Applied Intuition provides the multi-brand in-vehicle infotainment platform.

### Implementation Approach
- **Mistral Integration**: Generative AI across engineering and operations
- **Multi-brand Platform**: Applied Intuition enables brand customization
- **Innovation Lab**: Co-development with Mistral for proprietary solutions
- **Transformation Academy**: Training 270,000+ employees

### Key Business Outcomes
1. **Development**: 40% improvement in development efficiency
2. **Cycles**: 45% faster development cycles
3. **Quality**: 95% customer satisfaction
4. **Revenue**: $200M+ from accelerated launches

### Competitive Differentiation
- **Multi-brand Leadership**: Unified AI across 15 brands
- **Mistral Partnership**: European AI independence
- **Applied Intuition**: Advanced in-vehicle experience
- **Scale**: 3M+ vehicles annually

### Challenges Overcome
1. Integration across 15+ independent brands
2. Maintaining brand identity with unified platform
3. Global compliance across multiple regions

### Next Steps (2026)
- Autonomous vehicle AI platform
- Advanced driver assistance systems
- Fleet management AI
