## Detailed Narrative

### Strategic Context
Repsol partnered with Microsoft and Accenture to deploy enterprise-wide generative AI, launching ChatGPT enterprise capabilities in February 2025 and multi-agent platform in October 2025. The company has 670+ digital transformation initiatives underway, 400 supported by AI.

### Implementation Approach
- **Microsoft Copilot Deployment**: Enterprise-wide rollout with custom training
- **Multi-Agent Platform**: 22 AI agents deployed for planning, maintenance, incident resolution
- **Generative AI Competence Center**: 60+ use cases, 22 scaled operations
- **Workforce Training**: 5,000+ employees trained on AI tools

### Key Business Outcomes
1. **Process Automation**: 400+ digital cases supported by AI
2. **Efficiency**: 35% improvement in workflow execution
3. **Quality**: 40% reduction in manual errors
4. **Scalability**: 22 AI agents handling business-critical processes

### Competitive Differentiation
- **Multi-Agent Architecture**: Advanced autonomous workflow management
- **Rapid Deployment**: 6-month to enterprise scale
- **Industry Leadership**: First Spanish energy company at scale
- **Strategic Integration**: AI embedded in digital transformation

### Challenges Overcome
1. Enterprise-wide change management
2. AI governance at scale (5,000+ users)
3. Integration with legacy business systems

### Next Steps (2026)
- Advanced autonomous agent capabilities
- Industry-specific AI model development
- Competitive advantage through proprietary agents
