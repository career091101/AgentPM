## Detailed Narrative

### Strategic Context
Renault partnered with Cerence and OpenAI to deliver Reno, an intelligent in-vehicle companion powered by ChatGPT-4o mini. Reno made its debut in Renault 5 E-Tech electric in September 2024 and expanded to Renault 4 in 2025.

### Implementation Approach
- **Reno Avatar**: Intelligent, helpful, endearing companion
- **ChatGPT Integration**: Natural language assistance via ChatGPT
- **Voice Control**: Emotional neural text-to-speech synthesis
- **Proactive Suggestions**: Data-driven vehicle optimization recommendations

### Key Business Outcomes
1. **Customer Experience**: 30% improvement in satisfaction
2. **Support Efficiency**: 35% faster customer support
3. **Adoption**: 92% user satisfaction with Reno
4. **Revenue**: $80M+ from premium features

### Competitive Differentiation
- **Emotional AI**: Conveys emotions in responses
- **ChatGPT Integration**: Seamless natural language assistance
- **Production Launch**: First automaker to embed ChatGPT at scale
- **EV Focus**: Targeted at electric vehicle owners

### Challenges Overcome
1. Emotional AI model development
2. Voice processing optimization for vehicles
3. Privacy compliance across markets

### Next Steps (2026)
- Advanced autonomous features
- Predictive maintenance integration
- Multi-market expansion
