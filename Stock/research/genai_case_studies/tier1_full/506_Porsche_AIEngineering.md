## Detailed Narrative

### Strategic Context
Porsche Engineering developed AI-based tools to accelerate sports car development, achieving 50% reduction in specification refinement workload. Large language models handle technical document processing, while specialized ML models optimize vehicle performance across multiple dimensions.

### Implementation Approach
- **LLM Applications**: Natural language specification refinement
- **Driving Comfort**: AI evaluates objective ride comfort vs human experts
- **Crash Simulation**: AI accelerates FEM analysis (72 hours → minutes)
- **Battery Optimization**: Preventive anomaly detection for EV batteries

### Key Business Outcomes
1. **Development**: 50% reduction in specification workload
2. **Accuracy**: 95% accuracy in AI-assisted designs
3. **Speed**: 70% faster crash simulation
4. **Innovation**: Faster time-to-market for new models

### Competitive Differentiation
- **Premium Positioning**: AI for high-end vehicle development
- **Technical Excellence**: Specialized AI for engineering challenges
- **Development Speed**: Industry-leading acceleration
- **Quality Assurance**: Maintains premium quality standards

### Challenges Overcome

3. Precision requirements for sports car engineering
