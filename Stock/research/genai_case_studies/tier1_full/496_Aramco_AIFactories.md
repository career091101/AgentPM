## Detailed Narrative

### Strategic Context
Saudi Aramco is positioning Saudi Arabia as a global AI computing leader through a $90B investment package with NVIDIA, AWS, Qualcomm, and Groq. CEO Amin Nasser emphasized cheap natural gas and renewables as strategic advantages for AI infrastructure development.

### Implementation Approach
- **Well Productivity**: 2x improvement through AI-driven optimization
- **Plant Automation**: Autonomous operations at Fadhili Gas Plant (2.5B SCF/day)
- **AI Factories**: NVIDIA co-developed industrial AI computing infrastructure
- **Workforce Development**: Hiring thousands of data scientists
