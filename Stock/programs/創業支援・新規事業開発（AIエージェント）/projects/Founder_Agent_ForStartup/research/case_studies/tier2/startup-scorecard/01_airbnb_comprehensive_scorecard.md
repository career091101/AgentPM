### 4. Business Model (9/10) ✅

| Metric | Airbnb | VC Standard (ForStartup) | Evaluation |
|--------|--------|--------------------------|:----------:|
| LTV/CAC | **3-5x** | 5.0+ | ⚠️ Slightly below (but acceptable for marketplace) |
| CAC | $50-100 | Low as possible | ✅ Organic/referral-driven |
| LTV | $150-500 | High | ✅ Repeat bookings |
| CAC Payback | 3-6 months | 12 months max | ⭐⭐⭐ Exceptional |
| Gross Margin | 80%+ (service fees) | 70%+ (SaaS) | ⭐⭐⭐ Platform advantages |
| Monthly Growth Rate | 20-30% (2010-2012) | 20%+ | ✅ Meets standard |

**Revenue Model**:
- Host fee: 3% of booking value
- Guest service fee: 6-12% of booking value
- Total take rate: 9-15%

**Unit Economics Calculation**:
```
CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):
- Organic/SEO: $20-30
- Paid marketing: $50-100
- Referral program: $25 (two-way incentive)
- Weighted average: $50-70

LTV (Lifetime Value):
- Average booking value: $150
- Airbnb take rate: 10%
- Revenue per booking: $15
- Repeat bookings: 3-5x per year
- Customer lifetime: 3-5 years
- LTV: $15 × 4 × 4 = $240
- LTV/CAC: $240 / $60 = 4.0x
```

**Growth Metrics**:
- Nights booked (2010): 500K → (2011): 2M → (2012): 10M+
- Monthly growth rate: 25-30% (sustained over 3 years)
- ARR growth: 3x year-over-year

**Scorecard Impact**: 9/10 (strong unit economics, though LTV/CAC slightly below strict 5.0+ threshold - offset by marketplace advantages)

## Lessons for `/startup-scorecard` Skill

### 1. CPF Validation Depth Matters
- **Standard**: 30 interviews (VC minimum)
- **Airbnb**: 100+ in-person visits
- **Lesson**: Higher sample size → higher confidence in CPF score

### 2. LTV/CAC Nuance for Marketplaces
- **Airbnb**: 4.0x (below strict 5.0+ threshold)
- **Context**: Marketplace model has lower CAC due to network effects
- **Lesson**: Apply industry-specific standards (marketplaces: 3.0+ acceptable)

### 3. "Do Things That Don't Scale" Philosophy
- **Photo quality bottleneck**: Founders personally shot photos in NY
- **Result**: 2-3x booking increase
- **Lesson**: Manual intervention can unlock exponential growth

### 4. Mission-Driven Differentiation
- **"Belong Anywhere"** vs. "cheap accommodation"
- **Impact**: Premium positioning, higher LTV, brand loyalty
- **Lesson**: Mission clarity boosts PSF score (UVP 9/10)

### 5. Scorecard Evolution Over Time
- **2008** (Pre-Seed): 20/50 (idea validation)
- **2009** (Seed): 30/50 (YC acceptance, initial traction)
- **2010** (Series A): 40/50 (PMF achieved, 20%+ growth)
- **2011** (Series B): 45/50 (current analysis)
- **Lesson**: Scorecard should be recalculated at each funding stage
