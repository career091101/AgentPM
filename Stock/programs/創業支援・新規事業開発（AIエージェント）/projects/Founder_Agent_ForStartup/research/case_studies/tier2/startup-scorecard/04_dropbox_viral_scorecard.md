## Key Innovation: Referral Program

**Design**:
- Base storage: 2GB free
- Referral bonus: +500MB (both referrer and referee)
- Max: Up to 16GB free

**Results**:
- 35% of signups from referrals (permanent channel)
- Viral coefficient: 1.0-1.2 (every user brings 1+ users)
- CAC reduced from $300 (paid ads) to $5 (referrals)

**LTV/CAC Calculation**:
```
CAC: $5 (referral cost)
LTV: $500 (10% convert to paid $10/mo × 50 months)
LTV/CAC: 100:1 (exceptional)
```
