## 10x Advantage
- **Ease of Use**: 10x simpler (drag-and-drop vs. Photoshop's 100+ tools)

- **Price**: Free/$12.95/mo vs. Adobe $52.99/mo

## Lesson: Democratization Strategy
- Target: Non-designers (10x larger market than pro designers)
- Result: 100M+ users (vs. Adobe's 26M)
