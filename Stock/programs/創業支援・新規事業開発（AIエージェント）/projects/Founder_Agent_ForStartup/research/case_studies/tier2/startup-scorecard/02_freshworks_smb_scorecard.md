## Key Lessons for `/startup-scorecard`

### 1. Market Timing Can Boost CPF Score
- Freshworks launched during Zendesk's pricing controversy
- Lesson: External events can accelerate CPF validation

### 2. Domain Expertise (FIF) Is Critical
- Girish spent 9 years building helpdesk at Zoho
- Lesson: Prior experience in the domain significantly increases execution feasibility score

### 3. SMB vs. Enterprise Positioning
- Zendesk: Enterprise-focused ($50K+ deals)
- Freshworks: SMB-focused ($200-500/mo deals)
- Lesson: Segment selection affects CAC, LTV, and competitive dynamics

### 4. Freemium Model Trade-offs
- Pros: Low CAC, viral growth
- Cons: Lower LTV/CAC than pure paid models
- Lesson: Freemium is acceptable if LTV/CAC > 3.0x (Freshworks: 4.0x ✅)
