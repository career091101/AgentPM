## Pivot Analysis

**Original**: Consumer file storage (competing with Dropbox)
**Pivoted To**: Enterprise content management (avoiding Dropbox)

**Pivot Trigger**:
- Enterprise customers paying $10-50/user/month
- Consumers only paying $5/month
- 10x higher LTV in enterprise segment

**Scorecard Impact**:
- Pre-pivot: 28/50 (consumer market saturation)
- Post-pivot: 38/50 (enterprise opportunity +10 points)
