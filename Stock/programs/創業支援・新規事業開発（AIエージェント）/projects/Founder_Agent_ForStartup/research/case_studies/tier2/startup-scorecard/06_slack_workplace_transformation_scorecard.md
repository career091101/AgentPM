## Lessons for `/startup-scorecard`

### 1. DAU/MAU as PSF Indicator
- >50%: Good product-market fit
- >70%: Strong PMF
- >90%: Exceptional (Slack: 93%)

### 2. Network Effects in Team Tools
- 1 user: 0 value
- 10 users: Critical mass achieved
- 50+ users: Team lock-in

### 3. Freemium → Enterprise Upsell
- Free tier: Viral spread (CAC $0)
- Enterprise: High LTV ($5K+/year)
- LTV/CAC: 6-8x
